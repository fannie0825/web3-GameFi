Ecosystem Architecture

The Game Token (ERC-20)

The NOVA Token is the lifeblood of the economy. It is an infinitely divisible, fungible currency deployed via a smart contract.

  • • Minting: Tokens are dynamically minted by the protocol and sent to players' wallets upon winning matches.
  • • Utility: Required to purchase marketplace items or upgrade existing assets.
  • • Burning: Spent tokens are burned (removed from supply) to combat inflation.

Digital Assets (ERC-721)

Characters, weapons, and land are deployed as Non-Fungible Tokens. Every item is unique and provably owned by the player's wallet address.

  • • Interoperability: Because they are ERC-721 compliant, players can sell these assets on third-party markets like OpenSea.
  • • Metadata: Item stats (like level and class) are permanently stored in the token's metadata URI.
